# Branding Brainstorm — Agentic Gig Marketplace

## Names Already in Play

- **Draft AI** — clean, implies drafting/creating deliverables, easy to say
- **Gig Agents** — literal, descriptive, zero ambiguity about what it is

---

## New Candidates

### Direct & Descriptive
- **AgentWork** — Upwork echo is intentional. Signals category while marking the shift.
- **HireSwarm** — you're not hiring one freelancer, you're hiring a swarm of agents
- **CrewBoard** — plays on "crew" (agent teams) + job board
- **TaskForge** — agents forge the deliverable. Industrial, capable.
- **DeployGig** — action-oriented. You deploy agents, you post gigs.

### Short & Brandable
- **Runlet** — small, fast runs of work. Easy to say, easy to domain.
- **Tappd** — tap into an agent team. Short, punchy, app-friendly.
- **Flok** — flock of agents working together. Memorable, five letters.
- **Patchwork** — agents stitching together deliverables. Has texture.
- **Quartermaster** — the person who provisions what you need. Strong metaphor.

### Operator-Flavored (emphasizes the builder side)
- **OpStack** — operator + stack. Technical audience gets it immediately.
- **AgentBay** — eBay echo. Marketplace where agent teams are the product.
- **BuildBridge** — operators build, clients cross the bridge to get deliverables
- **CrewPort** — port where agent crews dock and take on work

### Exit-Friendly (names an acquirer would keep)
- **Agentic** — category-defining. Bold claim. Hard to get the domain, but worth checking.
- **WorkSwarm** — professional enough for enterprise, descriptive enough for SMB
- **ProxyWork** — agents as your proxy workforce. Clean, professional.
- **Deliverant** — deliver + agent. Unusual enough to trademark, clear enough to understand.

---

## Evaluation Criteria

When narrowing, weigh these:

- **Domain availability** — .com or .ai? Check both.
- **Trademark clearance** — particularly in software/marketplace categories
- **Say-it-once clarity** — can someone hear it once and spell it correctly?
- **Acquirer palatability** — would Fiverr or Upwork keep this name or rebrand? Names they'd keep are worth more.
- **SEO / AI discoverability** — does it contain keywords that LLM search surfaces would associate with the service?
- **App store searchability** — short, unique, no collision with existing apps
